Crypto Payment Disclaimer

Last updated: January 2025 · Please read carefully before sending any payment

⚠

Important — Read Before Sending Payment

Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible. Once you send USDT to our wallet address, the transaction cannot be reversed, recalled, or refunded under any circumstances. Please verify all details carefully before sending.

Accepted Payment Method

Fortiq Funded accepts one payment method only:

CurrencyUSDT (Tether)
NetworkTRC20 (TRON) only
Challenge Price$150 USDT
Minimum to Activate$148 USDT
Confirmations Required19 blockchain confirmations

We do not accept any other cryptocurrency, stablecoin, or payment method. We do not accept USDT on ERC20, BEP20, Solana, or any other network. Sending funds on the wrong network will result in permanent loss of those funds, and we cannot assist with recovery.

Payment Process

The payment and activation process works as follows:

  • You will be shown our USDT (TRC20) wallet address at checkout
  • Send exactly $150 USDT or more to that address from your own wallet
  • The system monitors the blockchain automatically for incoming transactions
  • Once 19 blockchain confirmations are detected and the amount meets the minimum threshold, your challenge is activated automatically
  • You will receive a notification when your challenge is activated
  • If you send less than $148 USDT, your challenge will not activate

Fee Tolerance

The advertised challenge price is $150 USDT. However, we recognise that cryptocurrency withdrawals from exchanges often incur network or withdrawal fees that reduce the amount received. To accommodate this, we will activate your challenge if we receive a minimum of $148 USDT.

This means:

  • Send $150 USDT or more → challenge activates normally
  • We receive $148 USDT or more → challenge still activates
  • We receive less than $148 USDT → challenge does not activate

If your challenge does not activate due to insufficient funds received, you must send the remaining amount to make up the difference, or contact our support team at support@fortiqfunded.com.

Irreversibility of Transactions

By making a payment, you fully acknowledge and accept that:

  • All cryptocurrency transactions are final and irreversible once confirmed on the blockchain
  • We cannot reverse, refund, or recover any payment once it has been sent
  • Challenge fees are non-refundable under all circumstances, including failure to pass the challenge, change of mind, or technical issues on your end
  • You are solely responsible for ensuring you send funds to the correct wallet address on the correct network
  • We are not liable for any funds sent to an incorrect address or on the wrong network

Wallet Address Verification

Always verify the wallet address shown at checkout carefully before sending any funds. Our wallet address will always be displayed directly on our Platform. We will never ask you to send funds to a wallet address provided via email, social media, or any channel other than the checkout page of our official Platform at fortiqfunded.com.

If you receive any communication asking you to send funds to an address not shown on our official checkout page, treat it as fraudulent and contact us immediately at support@fortiqfunded.com.

Payouts

All payouts to funded traders are also made exclusively in USDT on the TRC20 network. You must provide a valid TRC20 wallet address to receive your payout. We are not responsible for payouts sent to an incorrect or incompatible wallet address provided by you.

Risk Warning

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile. The value of USDT is designed to be stable, but the broader cryptocurrency ecosystem carries inherent risks including regulatory changes, technical failures, and market disruptions. Participation in the Fortiq Funded evaluation programme involves the non-refundable payment of a challenge fee, and there is no guarantee of passing the evaluation or receiving a payout.

Only participate with funds you can afford to lose. The challenge fee should be treated as a cost of participation, not an investment.
