Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · Fortiq Prop Digital

1. Introduction

Fortiq Prop Digital ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Fortiq Funded platform ("Platform"). By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Registration data: Full name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Payment data: Cryptocurrency wallet addresses and transaction hashes related to challenge purchases and payouts
  • Trading data: All trading activity, positions, profit/loss records, and account performance metrics
  • Technical data: IP address, device fingerprint, browser type, and login timestamps for security purposes
  • Communication data: Any correspondence you send to our support team
  • Referral data: Referral codes used during registration for affiliate programme tracking

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account on the Platform
  • To process challenge purchases and verify payments on the blockchain
  • To operate, monitor, and administer your trading accounts
  • To process payouts to eligible funded traders
  • To detect and prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse of the Platform
  • To verify affiliate referrals and process commission payments
  • To send transactional emails including account confirmation, OTP codes, and payout notifications
  • To respond to your support enquiries
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider with industry-standard encryption. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Two-factor authentication via OTP is required on every login
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security reviews are conducted

While we take all reasonable precautions to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating the Platform (such as our database and email providers), subject to confidentiality obligations
  • Where required by law, regulation, or court order
  • Where necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Fortiq Prop Digital, our users, or the public
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets, where your data may be transferred as part of that transaction

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Platform may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyse usage patterns. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the Platform.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell your browsing data to advertisers.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Trading records and payment data may be retained for up to 7 years for regulatory and audit purposes.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • The right to request deletion of your data, subject to our legal obligations
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected]. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from a user under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date. Your continued use of the Platform after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at [email protected].
